Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2025

AlienSignal ("we", "our", or "us") operates the-alien.net. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to operate the site:

  • Usage data: We count page visits in aggregate (no personal data stored — just a daily counter per day).
  • Log data: Cloudflare, our hosting provider, may collect standard web server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited. These logs are subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.

Cookies

AlienSignal does not use cookies or tracking technologies of its own. If we add third-party advertising (such as Google AdSense) in the future, those services may use cookies and this policy will be updated accordingly.

Third-Party Links

Every article on AlienSignal links to an external publisher's website. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before submitting any personal information.

Third-Party Services

  • Cloudflare Workers & D1: Our infrastructure provider. See Cloudflare Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: Used to load typography. Google may log font requests. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • PayPal: Optional donation link. PayPal handles all payment data. See PayPal Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

AlienSignal is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to access, modify, or delete. If you have questions about data processed by our infrastructure providers (Cloudflare), please contact them directly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: contact@the-alien.net
